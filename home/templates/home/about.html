About

GT Movies Store is a full-stack Django web application that lets users explore, search, and purchase movies online. The platform includes account registration, secure login, and a shopping cart system for managing movie orders. Users can also create, edit, and delete reviews to share feedback and help others make informed decisions. Built with the Django framework and a SQLite database, GT Movies Store delivers a responsive, user-friendly interface with detailed movie pages that include title, description, price, and images.
